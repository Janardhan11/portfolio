Work Experience

Deep Learning Intern – IIT Mandi

📍 Himachal Pradesh, India | 📅 June 2023 – October 2023

🛠 Technologies: Python, Deep Learning, TensorFlow, Google Colab

Overview

During my tenure as a Deep Learning Intern at IIT Mandi, I worked on cutting-edge AI models for image processing and quality assessment. My research focused on designing self-supervised learning techniques for image quality enhancement and developing efficient deep learning architectures for real-time evaluation of image distortions.

Key Contributions & Achievements

  • Developed an SSIM-Based Distortion Mapping Technique:
    • Analyzed over 6,000+ images to understand structural distortions using Structural Similarity Index (SSIM).
    • Created a distortion mapping pipeline that quantifies the level of degradation in images without requiring a reference.
    • This dataset was later used as the ground truth for training an unsupervised Convolutional Autoencoder (CAE) model.
  • Designed a CAE-Based No-Reference Image Quality Assessment Model:
    • Implemented a Convolutional Autoencoder (CAE) trained on distorted images to predict image quality scores.
    • The model achieved an impressive 0.02% loss rate, demonstrating high accuracy in detecting and assessing image quality.
    • Eliminated the need for reference images, making the system efficient, scalable, and applicable for real-time use in scenarios like medical imaging, satellite image enhancement, and autonomous vision systems.
  • Optimized Image Quality Assessment Process:
    • Improved the evaluation time by 60% by optimizing deep learning model architecture and leveraging TensorFlow optimizations.
    • Used GPU acceleration on Google Colab to enhance processing efficiency and enable faster inference.
    • The optimized system significantly improved the performance of image-based AI models, ensuring that they work effectively in real-world applications.

Impact & Learnings

  • ✅ Real-World Application: My work at IIT Mandi contributed to the development of no-reference image quality assessment models, which are crucial for applications in medical imaging, satellite imagery, and autonomous vision systems.
  • ✅ Optimization & Performance Engineering: I learned to optimize deep learning models for speed and accuracy, making them viable for real-time deployment on edge devices.
  • ✅ Advanced Deep Learning Concepts: Gained hands-on experience with Autoencoders, Self-Supervised Learning, Image Enhancement, and Distortion Analysis techniques.
